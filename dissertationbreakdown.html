Description

During this project I learnt a great deal, varying from, planning and project management techniques, to new programming tools. This was the first project of this scale which I had ever tackled. I had to account for the whole process from planning to implementation, and alongside this document everything along the way.

Adopting a "SCRUM" methodology allowed me to incrementally work on requirements, this allowed for my personal breakthrough of structuring everything around the functional requirements which I had uncovered during the research process. Once organised these requirements would form the backbone of everything in the project and allowed me to focus on purely implementation. This gave my project a very logical flow, and in combination with a Gantt chart and a project diary, it allowed me to keep to a strict deadline.

Aside from these techniques, I learnt some more abstract skills including, learning how to break a large project down to more manageable sub-goals and tracking progress to both report back to my supervisor but, also to stay motivated. Overall, this project opened my eyes to what I'm capable of and taught me a lot of valuable lessons.
