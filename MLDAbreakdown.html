Retail Rocket Analysis and Recommender

Date: 05/11/2020 - 29/04/21

Description:This was a group project, for privacy reasons I have removed people’s names and student numbers from the work shown. This project involved taking an existing data set known as “the retail rocket dataset” and performing various data analysis techniques and machine learning methods. These allowed us to come to conclusions regarding the customer behaviour and buying patterns, which would then inform 4 models to recommend items to customers.

During this project I learnt about how to structure a data analysis report, using Jupyter notebooks, and most importantly, team organisation. I took a lead role in organising and structure my group, including allocation of tasks, scheduling meetings, and chasing individuals up who hadn’t completed their tasks. Alongside this, I completed all data cleaning, exploration, and visualisations sections, as well as creating 3 out of the 4 recommender models. From this project I greatly improved my skills with python as well as my ability to problem solve within the machine learning field, which I would describe myself as lacking knowledge.
